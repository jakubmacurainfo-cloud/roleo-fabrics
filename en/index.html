About us

ROLEO expands into fabrics

Roleo Fabrics is a new division of the ROLEO brand focused on distributing fabrics for window covering systems. We build our own collections, codes and sales materials for B2B partners.

Who is it for?

For pleated blind manufacturers, installers, showrooms, window covering distributors and companies that want to expand their offer with honeycomb fabrics.
